Seminar area

The seminar area of the studio is for flexible use and supports audio-visual presentations independent from the 25.4 loudspeaker array.

Equipment includes:

  • 65" Sony Bravia 4K TV display FW-65BZ35F, wall mounted
  • 2 Genelec 8050B studio monitors
  • Crestron 4K DigitalMedia Presentation System DMPS3-4K-150-C 
  • Extron DMP 44LC, 4x4 digital matrix processor
  • Mackie 1202VLZ4 12 channel mixer
  • Video inputs, rack: HDMI, VGA
  • Audio inputs, rack: HDMI, 3.5mm, balanced XLR (line-level)
  • AV outputs, rack: HDMI, Assitive Listening System 
  • Dry-erase, magnetic presentation boards 
  • 8 HermanMiller Flip-Top tables
  • 18 HermanMiller Caper chairs
  • 20 stools
