Spatial audio studio, rules and guidelines
    • Use of the studio must be scheduled using the reservation system.
    • Use of the studio is restricted to students enrolled in hosted courses and the associated faculty. Access is granted after an introductory training.
    • Do not move or reposition the 25.4 array loudspeakers in any way. The system is calibrated to exact posisitons.
    • This is a clean room. The allowed type of work is computer-based production. No materials or tools that produce dust or fumes are allowed.
    • Do not hang anything from the ceiling grid or speakers.
    • Do not pin, tape, or mount anything in any way to the walls or acoustic panels.
    • Do not leave or store anything in this room. Items left will be dicarded.
    • Installations require special permission from studio management.
    • The tables and chairs in the seminar area can be rearranged to accomodate usage needs. Please return to "default" state.
    • No eating in the studio. Beverages with lids in seminar area are OK. Keep drinks away from computers and equipment.
    • Work conducted in the studio may not cause or threaten physical harm to its occupants, equipment, or facilities.

Failure to adhere to these policies can result in revoked access.
